## Features Section

**Headline:**  
Everything You Need to Keep Your Recipes Safe

**Feature 1: Smart Scanning**  
Take a picture. Our AI reads the recipe and saves it. Works with photos, PDFs, and even messy handwriting.

**Feature 2: Add Your Own Photos**  
Upload pictures of each cooking step. Add a photo of the finished dish. Make your recipes come alive.

**Feature 3: Organized by Category**  
Appetizers. Main dishes. Desserts. Soups. Snacks. Find any recipe in seconds.

**Feature 4: Remember Where It Came From**  
Tag each recipe with its source. Mom. Grandma. A friend. A magazine. Never forget where the recipe started.

**Feature 5: Build Collections**  
Group your favorites. Create a "Mom's Recipes" collection. Or "Holiday Baking." Or "Quick Weeknight Dinners."

**Feature 6: Create a Recipe Book**  
Turn your collection into a beautiful PDF. Print it. Share it. Pass it on.
